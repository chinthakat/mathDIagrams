
# MathDiagram AI - 2D Mathematics Diagram Engine Requirements

## Purpose

Build a professional 2D mathematics diagram creation platform capable of generating diagrams required for:

* ICAS Mathematics
* NAPLAN Numeracy
* ACER Scholarship Tests
* Selective School Entrance Exams
* Opportunity Class (OC) Tests
* Primary School Mathematics Assessments
* Middle School Mathematics Assessments

Target coverage:

* Years 2–8
* 95%+ of all mathematics diagrams appearing in assessments

---

# Product Goals

## Primary Goal

Allow teachers, tutors, content creators, and AI systems to create publication-quality mathematics diagrams within seconds.

## Secondary Goal

Support AI-driven diagram generation from natural language prompts.

Example:

> Draw an isosceles triangle ABC where AB = AC and angle A = 40°.

---

# MVP Coverage Goal

The MVP must support:

* Geometry
* Fractions
* Number lines
* Measurement
* Data & graphs
* Coordinate geometry

This should cover approximately 80–90% of diagrams appearing in ICAS, NAPLAN and Scholarship mathematics assessments for Years 2–8.

# Success Criteria

A content creator should be able to reproduce any diagram from:

* ICAS Mathematics Years 2–8
* NAPLAN Numeracy Years 3, 5, 7
* ACER Scholarship Tests
* Selective School Mathematics Tests

without requiring external drawing software.
